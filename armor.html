In Perfect World your character is protected by armor. Armor consists of three basic types: Arcane Armor, Light Armor, and Heavy Armor.

Within each type of armor multiple levels of armor exist. What level of armor you can use is dependent upon your character level and attributes.

Each level of armor has multiple pieces, each of which cover different body parts. Four pieces directly change the look of your character. These pieces are: Chest, Arm, Leg, and Boots. Helmets and Robes are armor items that do not change the appearance of your character.
